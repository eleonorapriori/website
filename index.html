About

I am a researcher at the Centro Studi e Documentazione Luigi Einaudi, Turin and Contract Professor of Applied Economics and Financial Science at the University of Turin. My research focuses on agent-based modelling, complexity economics, gender economics, and methodological issues in economic simulation.

Research Interests

  • Agent-Based Modelling in Economics
  • Complex Economic Systems
  • Gender Economics
  • Production Networks and Macroeconomic Dynamics
  • Economic Methodology
  • Inequality, Labour Markets, and Technological Change
